Definition 1.13.9. Primes and irreducibles.

Let \(R\) be a subring of \(\C\text{,}\) and let \(\alpha\) be a nonzero non-unit of \(R\text{:}\) i.e., \(\alpha\in R-(R^*\cup \{0\})\text{.}\)
  • \(\alpha\) is irreducible if \(\alpha=\beta\gamma\) implies \(\beta\in R^*\) \(\gamma\in R^*\text{.}\)
    🔗
    🔗
  • \(\alpha\) is prime if \(\alpha\mid \beta\gamma\) implies \(\alpha\mid \beta\) or \(\alpha\mid \gamma\text{.}\)
